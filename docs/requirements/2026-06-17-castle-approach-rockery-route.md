# Castle Approach Rockery Route

## Context And Goal

The route from the spawn area to the castle is too flat and direct. It should become a compact but winding Dark Souls-like approach with sharp rockery barriers and visual obstruction.

## Success Criteria

- The player must follow a curved route rather than walking directly to the castle.
- Rock formations are high enough and steep enough to block traversal.
- The rockery reads as continuous stone mass, not scattered low mounds or loose cones.
- The route remains passable and does not block the castle entrance.

## Planned Changes

- Replace the flat approach with a winding path bounded by sharp rockery/stone ridges.
- Use continuous polyhedral rock mass geometry instead of separated rocks with gaps.
- Apply appropriate stone texture treatment to match the dark-fantasy environment.
- Remove conflicting default stones from the route corridor.

## Verification Plan

- Walk from spawn to castle and confirm the route is curved but passable.
- Attempt to climb rock barriers and confirm they block traversal.
- Visually inspect that the rockery reads as one continuous mass.
- Run the project build after implementation.

- Later small distance adjustments to rockery placement remain in conversation history.
